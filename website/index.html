Our Services

Crafted by healthcare professionals, tech pros and our customers’ feedback; we firmly believe in improving people’s lives by offering intutive and seemless digital products. Starting with the patient as our core user, Yodawy provides fully integrated solutions that close the gap between different stakeholders in the healthcare sector.

PHARMACY DELIVERY APP

A user-friendly mobile app enabling users to easily search and order any medicine or personal care item, as well as order their insurance claims, and have them delivered immediately from the best-matching pharmacy. Users can also upload a product or prescription picture for added convienece.

DIGITAL PRESCRIPTION APP

The result of working closely with doctors to digitize prescription writing. Gone are the days of searching for an old prescription or trying to read one. Doctors now can easily write, send and save electronic prescriptions to their patients through Yodawy’s digital Prescription App. A true masterpiece!

INSURANCE APPROVALS ENGINE

An automated artificial intelligence real-time approval engine localized to the market needs. Forget about fraud, hours to receive approvals or implementing new provider systems. Yodawy’s intelligent algorithm offers state-of-the-art tech offering a lightning fast efficient experience.

PHARMACY AND PARTNERS PORTAL

Built from the ground up with pharmacy operations in mind. A dedicated dashboard for receiving orders in a simple and quick processing method. We understand the hectic environments that operators are faced with everyday. Our portal is designed for speed, agility and accuracy.
